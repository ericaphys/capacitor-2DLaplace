# capacitor-2DLaplace
Finite differences simulation of a parallel plate capacitor without dielectrics inside, in a 2D space. The 2D Laplace equation in free space is being solved.
Graphs for potential, electric field, charge on plates are being plotted. Charge shows numerical instabilty at the edges of the plates. The estimated capacitance
from the simulation is in the same order of magnitude of the theoretical one.
